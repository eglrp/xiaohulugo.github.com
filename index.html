About Me (CV)

I am a master student of the third year in School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering at Wuhan University supervised by Prof. Jian Yao. My research interests lie in the areas of computer vision. I have done some work on: (1) image feature extraction and matching, image segmentation and image stitching; (2) panorama processing; (3) point cloud segmentation; (4) data clustering. In particular, I am now interested in visual reality and working on panoramic video stitching.
